PICBOX

Automatically save photos and videos you liked on Instagram to your Dropbox

Just link your Instagram and Dropbox accounts, and use Instagram app as you always do. Your likes will be downloaded to your Dropbox folder.

Farewell Time for Picbox
Due to platform changes announced by Instagram, Picbox will not be able to operate starting from June 1, 2016. Already saved likes to your Dropbox won't be affected by this.
For anyone interested in improving, modifying the product to a better state, I'm publishing the source code on Github.
